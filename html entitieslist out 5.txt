2)What are html entities?list out 5 commonly used html entities.
ans->   HTML entities are the reserved characters that are used in the HTML document . They are not present in your standard keyboard. They provide a wide range of characters, allowing you to add icons, geometric shapes, mathematical operators, and so on
The 5 commonly used html entities are following:-
1. &	&amp;	Interpreted as the beginning of an entity or character reference.
2. "	&quot;	Interpreted as the beginning and end of an attribute's value.	I
3. ©	&copy;	Interpreted as the copyright sign.
4. ®	&reg;	Interpreted as the registered sign.
5. ™	&trade;	Interpreted as the trademark sign
